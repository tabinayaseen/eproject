Park Info

Fun Zone is a theme park with its ideal location near Biograd na Moru offering a spectacular entertainment experience for the whole family in the heart of the Adriatic coast. Choose your adventure from one of three driving themes: Space, Pirate City or Wild West and experience fun in an unprecedented way. Besides our rides, you will be entertained by magicians, jugglers, circus performers, stand-up comedians and similar exhibitionists from various parts of the world. Nobody can stay hungry or thirsty next to numerous catering facilities that will surely satisfy the needs of all visitors. Special arrangements, weddings, team-building and birthdays can also be organized on a special occasion for a spectacular experience at Fun Zone. Our management pays special attention to the security of all individuals because we want your time spent in our amusement park to be an unforgettable positive experience that you will tell everybody.
